Bergen on a budget

Restaurants

It is not common to go out for dinner very often in Norway. You might think that the reason is that restaurants are usually quite expensive (which they are), but that's not the real reason. According to the typical Norwegian mentality, you are not supposed to show or think that you are better than anyone else, or to show that you have lots of money. This has to do with the Law of Jante. That's why most Norwegians don't really go out to eat often, but they actually do it when there is something to celebrate or it is a special occasion.

Although it's not common, there's always nice to have in mind a few places to go celebrate a birthday or other events, or just enjoy a nice dinner with your new exchange friends. Below we leave you three recommendations of cheap restaurants where you could get some really nice food for a reasonable price.

Recommendations

Sabrura

  • Description: sushi and fried asian food restaurant that offers a good deal with it's all-you-can-eat buffet. You can also order different sushi plates from the menu, but we strongly recommend going there for the buffet.
  • Price range: for the buffet
    • Lunch (until 2pm): 199 NOK
    • Dinner (after 2pm): 229 NOK
  • Location: there's two of them in two different malls in Bergen, one is in Galleriet and you can find the other one in Bergen Storsenter.
  • Opening hours:
    • Monday to Saturday: 10am - 11pm
    • Sunday: 12am - 11pm
  • Phone number: +47 401 06 310
  • Website: sabrurasushi.no

Daily Pot

  • Description: small restaurant located 5 minutes away from the city center specialized in bowls of soup. You first choose the size of the bowl, then the type of soup you want and then, depending on the size of the bowl, you have a certain amount of toppings that you can add to it. It's a really nice and cozy place to get lunch on a cold day.
  • Price range: 89 NOK - 159 NOK
  • Location: Vaskerelven 21, 5014 Bergen, Norway
  • Opening hours:
    • Monday to Friday: 11am - 6pm
    • Saturday and Sunday: 12am - 6pm
  • Phone number: +47 450 75 373
  • Website: dailypot.no

Pizzabakeren

  • Description: perfect place to either go get some pizza with your friends or have it delivered to your house. It's affordable and pretty good quality.
  • Price range: 41 NOK - 223 NOK
  • Location: Baneveien 13, 5010 Bergen, Norway
  • Opening hours:
    • Sunday to Thursday: 11am - 10pm
    • Friday and Saturday: 11am - 11pm
  • Phone number: +47 55 32 44 40
  • Website: pizzabakeren.no
