Bergen on a budget

Gyms

Working out is a part of many people's weekly life, and this is not exluding students. There are a lot of students in Bergen, and there are quite many of them that work out regular basis. Therefore it is important to showcase all the different most affordable fitness centers within a fair distance from the city center.

How do we decide which package has the best value?

What do we mean by best value? We mean the product that retain as much of its quality as possible while still being affordable. The way the best value package was chosen in the Gym section, was that their prices were analyzed, and compared it to what you get within that package compared to the other packages available. So the best alternative may not be the cheapest in the long run. Whether you are styaing here for a semester, or longer, that is also taken in to consideration for the best value.
