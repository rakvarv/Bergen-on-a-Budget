Clothing shops

You might have heard that Bergen has a vast variety of wheather, changing in quick succession, from a nice sunny wheather to a massive hail of rain lasting for hours. Because of these viarying wheather conditions, having the proper clothing can be really benefitial. Even if you are going out with friends, going out for fine dining, or even going to a party, having the right clothing can be a gamechanger. We have a list of clothing stores with a vide variety of clothing, with a mostly fairly low price-range.

Recommendations

Sports Outlet

  • Description: Sports Outlet is a clothing store that mainly focuses on clothing for outdoor activites. All from running to fishing clothing and gear is provided here. Even if you need a new fishing rod, they have it. If you want yourself a new t-shirt for working out, they most likely will have that as well. Prices vary from 100 NOK to 550 NOK, and very rarely going above this range.
