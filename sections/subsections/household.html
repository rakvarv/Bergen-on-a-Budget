Household goods shops

You probably arrived to your room at Fantoft or other student housing and you found out that you were either missing kitchenware, bed sheets, a pillow or maybe a study lamp. For all this things, we put some places where you can get all that stuff that you need.

We strongly suggest that you explore all the second-hand shops or flea markets around the city, that's one of the best ways to get really unexpensive things that maybe you needed. Also, you should definitely check the website finn.no, it works just like eBay and usually there's many people giving away things they don't need anymore.

Recommendations

Byttebua Bergen

  • Description: exchange market for UiB students and employees. Here you can pick up anything that you see and like for free. Also, you can leave stuff that you don't need anymore or that you cannot bring back home with you so other students can use it next year.
  • Location: Nina Griegs gate 2, 5015 Bergen, Norway (entering the gate, the door is all the way across on the left).
  • Opening hours: Tuesdays and Thurdays: 4pm - 6pm
  • Phone number: they don't have one.
  • Website: they do not have a website, but they use their instagram @byttebuabergen to show all the new things they get and when they open.
